Offer-Bridge Saver

This extension adds a floating button on Amazon, Flipkart & Myntra product pages so you can send product data directly to Offer-Bridge.

✓ Amazon.in   ✓ Flipkart.com   ✓ Myntra.com

Go to any product page on Amazon, Flipkart or Myntra and click the "Send to Offer-Bridge" button that appears at the bottom-right of the screen.
